Medusa (Μέδουσα)

Mortal Gorgon

Daughters of the sea gods Phorcys and Ceto

Sisters- Euryale and Stheno (Born as one of 3 gorgon sisters)

Eventually Medusa is murdered by Perseus -Son of Zeus and Danae-in her sleep. (Ordered by King Polydectes, helped by the gods, cut off her head.) Results in the birth of Chrysaor and the winged horse Pegasus. Usually, they are depicted as children of Poseidon however why that is differs. Either they were born from drops of Medusa's blood that fell into the sea or, more commonly known, from Medusa's neck when she was beheaded.

Perseus gained favor of the gods and wore a cap of Invisibility (maybe Hades’) and Hermes - winged sandals and sword, Athena – Mirror Sheild. Kept medusa’s head and where it ended up.
